# Future Improvements - Ashes of Aeloria

## World Rebuilding Mechanics
1. **Settlement Construction System**: Design and build cities from ruins with resource management and planning
2. **Population Dynamics**: Manage survivors, refugees, and new births with morale and needs systems
3. **Technology Recovery**: Rediscover lost technologies through exploration, research, and ancient archives
4. **Environmental Restoration**: Heal corrupted lands through magical or technological means
5. **Cultural Renaissance**: Restore art, music, literature, and traditions of the fallen civilization

## Hero & Character Development
6. **Legacy Heroes**: Multiple generations of heroes with inherited abilities and family storylines
7. **Moral Alignment System**: Complex morality affecting story outcomes and character relationships
8. **Skill Mastery Trees**: Deep progression systems with multiple specialization paths
9. **Companion Relationships**: Rich relationship systems with romance, friendship, and rivalry options
10. **Character Backstory Generator**: Procedural backstory creation affecting starting abilities and connections

## Exploration & Discovery
11. **Procedural Ruins**: Randomly generated dungeon-like ruins with unique layouts and treasures
12. **Archaeological System**: Piece together historical events through artifact discovery and analysis
13. **Hidden Societies**: Discover surviving enclaves with their own cultures, politics, and resources
14. **Ancient Mysteries**: Solve complex puzzles about what caused the apocalypse and how to prevent recurrence
15. **Dynamic Weather**: Harsh environmental conditions affecting travel, combat, and resource gathering

## Political & Social Systems
16. **Faction Management**: Lead or ally with different survivor factions with competing ideologies
17. **Trade Networks**: Establish trade routes between settlements for resource exchange
18. **Council Governance**: Democratic decision-making systems affecting kingdom development
19. **Justice System**: Create and enforce laws with consequences for player and NPC actions
20. **Cultural Events**: Festivals, ceremonies, and traditions that boost morale and unity among survivors
